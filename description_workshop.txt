[b]Miscellanaria[/b] is a mod that aims to bring old and unreleased Terraria items, mechanics and features back, reworked and rebalanced for modern Terraria.

[b]This includes:[/b]
[list]
[*][b]First Fractal[/b] - an original endgame Terraria sword
[*][b]Luminite Axes, Hammers and Chainsaws[/b]
[*][b]Scythe, Soul Scythe and Icemourne[/b]
[*][b]Skull Bow[/b]
[*][b]Key Molds[/b]
[*][b]Unimplemented boss bags[/b]
[*][b]Color-Only Dye[/b]
[*][b]Orb of Light pet[/b]
[*][b]White Cultist banners[/b]
[*][b]Dreadnautilus Trophy[/b]
[b]and more![/b]
[/list]

This mod doesn't significantly affect the balance of vanilla game, so you can play it alongside other, larger content mods, such as [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2909886416]Thorium[/url] or [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2824688072]Calamity[/url] without any problems.
